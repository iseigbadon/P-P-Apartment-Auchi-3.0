## How to Use New Features

### Favorite Apartments
1. Click the heart button on any apartment card
2. Heart will turn solid red with emoji feedback
3. Favorites persist across sessions

### Search & Book
1. Click "Where" to select location
2. Click "When" to pick check-in/out dates
3. Click "Who" to set guest count
4. Click search button to view apartments
5. Click any apartment to open booking modal
6. Fill in details and click WhatsApp to book

### Share Experiences
1. Scroll to Guest Experiences section
2. Enter your name (optional)
3. Share your experience text
4. Upload a photo (optional)
5. Click "Share Experience"
6. Your review appears on the website

### Contact & Support
- Click phone numbers to call
- Use WhatsApp button for quick contact
- View location on embedded map
- Follow social media links
